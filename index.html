What is the OTIF Order Release Score?

The On-Time In-Full (OTIF) Order Release metric measures our success in releasing customer orders exactly as planned. An order must meet two critical conditions to pass: it must be released on the scheduled date (On Time) and all its items must be fulfilled completely (In Full). This single score reflects our operational reliability and commitment to customer satisfaction.

On Time

In Full

What's Excluded?

To ensure a fair and relevant measurement, certain order types are intentionally excluded from the OTIF calculation.

Order Type Reason for Exclusion
Internal Orderlines Stock transfers; not an external customer-facing order.
Assemblage Orders Component parts for kits; follow a separate production schedule.
Specials Items, which are not released via SLIM4, and can therefore also not be measured.
